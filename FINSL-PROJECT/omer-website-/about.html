About Omer

Omer Kochba is a violinist, producer, and sonic explorer from Israel, currently based in Boston, where he is studying Electronic Production and Design at Berklee College of Music. Blending tradition with innovation, Omer creates immersive soundscapes using his electric violin, a loop-based pedalboard setup, and a deep curiosity for genre-crossing experimentation.

Drawing from a wide range of influences—including Arab music, jazz, IDM, drum and bass, ambient, and experimental electronic styles—his music blurs the lines between acoustic intimacy and digital abstraction. Whether performing live or producing in the studio, Omer aims to create emotionally resonant experiences that invite reflection, movement, and a sense of connection.

His artistic vision is rooted in both technical exploration and cultural storytelling, as he seeks to honor his musical heritage while pushing the boundaries of modern sound design.
